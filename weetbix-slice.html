Weetbix Slice

Ingrediants

  • 150g butter - melted
  • 4 weetbix
  • 1/2 C Sugar
  • 1 Tb Cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • vanilla essence
  • 1 C flour

    • Icing

  • 1 1/2 C icing sugar
  • 1/4 C cocoa powder
  • Hot Water
  • 1/2 C dessicated coconut

Method

  1. Crush weetbix and mix with sugar, cocoa, baking powder and flour
  2. Stir in melted butter and vanilla essence
  3. press firmly into slice tin
  4. bake for 10mins at 180degrees C
  5. while baking prepare icing
  6. combine cocoa and icing sugar
  7. add hot water until reaching desired consistency
  8. pour icing over hot slice after baking
  9. sprinkle with dessicated coconut
  10. allow to cool completely before slicing and serving
  11. Enjoy!
