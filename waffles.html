Waffles

Ingrediants

  • 2 eggs - seperated
  • 1 1/2 C milk
  • 2 C flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 C sugar
  • 60g butter -melted and cooled, plus extra greasing
  • 2 Tb cold water

Method

  1. firmly beat eggwhites and set aside
  2. beat egg yolks and milk together
  3. Mix in sifted dry ingrediants,then add butter and water
  4. fold in beaten egg whites
  5. pre-heat waffle iron, then cook waffles until golden. Use approx 2 Tb batter per waffle.
  6. Top with favourite toppings
  7. Enjoy!
