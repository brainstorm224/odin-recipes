Pancakes

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 C flour
  • pinch salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Tb oil
  • 3/4 C milk
  • vanilla essence

method

  1. sift flour and salt into bowl, add eggs and oil, and mix into a very smooth batter
  2. Gradually add milk, batter will have a thin consistency
  3. heat pan and grease with butter
  4. cook pancakes, adding butter between each one
  5. top with you favourite pancake toppings
  6. Enjoy!
